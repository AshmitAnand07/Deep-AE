# Design System: Deep-AE Modern AI Platform

## Overview & Creative North Star
A sleek, futuristic, and highly professional AI dashboard designed for acoustic structural health monitoring. It embraces a premium "Dark Mode Glassmorphism" aesthetic.

## Colors
- **Primary**: Neon Cyan (`#00C9FF`) to represent high-tech analysis and waveforms.
- **Secondary**: Slate Gray (`#94a3b8`) for secondary text and muted elements.
- **Background**: Deep Space Blue (`#050810`) for maximum contrast.
- **Surfaces**: Semi-transparent dark surfaces with backdrop blur to simulate glass cards.

## Typography
- **Primary Font**: Inter. Clean, highly legible, and modern.

## Shapes & Radii
- Generously rounded corners (16px to 20px) to make the UI feel approachable despite the complex data.

## Animation
- Fluid, continuous zero-gravity drifting animations to make the interface feel alive and immersive.
