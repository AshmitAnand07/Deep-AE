2. Project Overview
Deep-AE is an AI-powered acoustic monitoring system designed to detect possible structural damage by
analyzing sound patterns generated from materials under stress.
The system captures acoustic signals using microphones or acoustic sensors and processes them using
Deep Learning models.
The AI model classifies structural conditions into categories such as:
Healthy
Crack Detected
Severe Crack
Environmental Noise
The primary focus of Phase 1 is to build and validate the core AI model.

Develop a CNN-based audio classification system capable of:
processing acoustic signals,

and classifying structural conditions in real time.

8. Success Criteria
The project will be considered successful if:
The CNN model achieves acceptable classification performance.
The model can differentiate crack sounds from noise.
The preprocessing pipeline works consistently.
The prediction system correctly classifies unseen samples.
